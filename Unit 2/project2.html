According to dol.gov, "The first Labor Day holiday was celebrated on Tuesday, September 5, 1882".

  • Things to know about it's origin:
    1. It was first rooted in the late 19th century
    2. Made to recognize the efforts of workers toward the strength and prosperity of America
    3. Became recognized by labor activists and some individual states before it was passed as a federal holiday
  • Who where the founder's of Labor Day?
    1. Peter J. McGuire
