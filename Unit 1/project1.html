Kalli's Homeade Caesar Salad Dressing

The tastiest salad dressing you will ever lay your hands on! Simple and easy to make in just 5 minutes!

This recipe has quickly become a favorite in my house, and I am so excited to share it with you!
With simple ingredients that most people can find in their kitchen, you too can make this delicious Homeade Caesar Salad Dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of Mayonaise
  • 1 Medium Garlic Clove, minced
  • 2 tsp Lemon Juice, to taste
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp of Worcestershire Sauce, to taste
  • 1/8 tsp of Salt
  • 1/3 cup of finely grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tbsp of Water
  • A pinch of Black Pepper, to taste

Procedure:

  1. Using a medium size bowl, add in your
    mayonaise, lemon juice, dijon mustard,
    worcestershire sauce, and water.
  2. Mix together the wet ingredients until
    thoroughly combined.
  3. Once combined, add in your garlic, salt,
    and black pepper.
  4. Mix in the seasonings until they are distributed
    evenly throughout the dressing.
  5. Finally, add in your parmesan cheese and mix
    until everything is combined and your dressing is
    a medium viscosity.
  6. Taste test your dressing to see if any adjustments
    need to be made, to your liking.
  7. Pour over your favorite salad, pasta, or anything
    you like and enjoy!
